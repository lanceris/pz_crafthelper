description=[*]Keybind to close active tab (None by default)
description=[*]Ability to change window opacity
description=[*]Favorites presets
description=[*]Help tooltip with current keyboard shortcuts
description=[*]Item favorites now stored in `CHC_item_favorites` table in player modData
description=[*]Textures now loaded on demand (i.e. when shown on UI) instead of at startup
description=[*]Item attributes now loaded on demand (i.e when shown on UI/searched by attribute ($))
description=[*]Ability to hold button to traverse item/recipe lists (delay is configurable)
description=[*]Improved rendering for large lists of items (only part of objects shown are processed, instead of all)
description=[*]Reduced mod initialization time by ~4.5-5 times by not loading all textures/attributes on startup
description=[*]Fixed book teached recipes not showing correctly when game language is not English
description=[*]Fixed crash when some textures for vanilla items are missing
description=[*]Fixed incorrect icons for moveables
